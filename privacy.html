WeMap – Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 31, 2025

1. Introduction

WeMap respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

2. Data Collection

WeMap does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

  • Location Data: Used only within the app to show your position on the map and calculate distances/bearings. Location data is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Imported Files: Any KML/KMZ files you import and any edits you make are saved locally on your device. They are not uploaded or transmitted outside your phone.

3. Data Sharing

WeMap does not share your data with any third parties. All data stays on your device unless you choose to export and share it yourself.

4. Third-Party Services

WeMap uses Google Maps SDK for displaying maps. Google may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, security is handled by the operating system (iOS). WeMap does not transmit or store your data externally.

6. Your Choices

You may delete imported files or uninstall the app at any time to remove all stored data.
